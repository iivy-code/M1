Part 3 — Reading Comprehension 10 points

School Life in Australia

In Australia, most students start school when they are five years old. The school day usually begins at about 9 am and finishes at around 3.30 pm. Students often wear a school uniform, and many schools ask students to wear a hat outside because of the strong sun.

Lessons are 50 minutes long, and students have a short break in the morning and a longer lunch break at midday. Popular subjects include English, maths, science and sport — swimming is very common because the weather is warm for most of the year.

After school, many students do sports training, play outside with friends, or do homework before dinner. The school year in Australia is different from many other countries: it starts in late January and finishes in December, with a long summer holiday over Christmas.
